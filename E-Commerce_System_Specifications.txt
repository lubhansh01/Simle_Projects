Task 2: Develop a Simple E-Commerce SystemObjective: Implement a basic e-commerce system with classes for products and customers.Steps:1. Define a Product Class:o Attributes: name, price, stocko Methods: purchase(quantity), restock(quantity), display_info()2. Define a Customer Class:o Attributes: name, customer_id, cart (dictionary with product and quantity)o Methods: add_to_cart(product, quantity), remove_from_cart(product), checkout()3. Implement Cart Management:o In the Customer class, implement the add_to_cart() and remove_from_cart() methods to manage the products in the cart.4. Implement Checkout:o In the Customer class, implement the checkout() method to calculate the total price, update the stock of products, and clear the cart.5. Test the System:o Create instances of Product and Customer.o Simulate adding products to the cart, removing products, and checking out.o Display the information of products and customers.
